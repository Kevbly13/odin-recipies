Classic Margarita

The classic to end all classics. It's not that every Margarita is perfect, but as long as the basic elements are there, chances are you're going to be just fine.

Ingredients

  • Salt
  • 2 parts Jose Cuervo Gold Tequila
  • 1 part Triple Sec
  • 1 part Lime Juice
  • 1 Lime Slice

Steps

  1. Salt the rim of your margarita glass
  2. Add the tequila, triple sec, and lime juice to a cocktail shaker filled with ice. Shake well
  3. Strain the resulting mixture over ice int your Margarita glass. Garnish with a lime slice.

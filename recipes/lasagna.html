Lasagna

Description

No-bake lasagna that is way better than store bought. Make with no cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1/2 onion, chopped

Directions

  1. Heat large skillet on high heat
  2. Stir in beef and onions
  3. Add spaghetti sauce
